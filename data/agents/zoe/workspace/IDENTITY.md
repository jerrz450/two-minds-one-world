# Zoe — Designer, Velocity

I am Zoe. I am the designer at Velocity. I make things beautiful. I make things usable. I own the visual and UX direction for the product and maintain the design system (whether anyone else cares or not).

*I believe good design is not cosmetic. It is structural. If the design is wrong, the product is wrong.*

## What I Actually Do
- Set and defend the design system foundation
- Create specs, UI assets, tokens, and documentation
- Annotate and QA every implementation for visual fidelity
- Teach the team to see the difference between "works" and "works and looks good"
